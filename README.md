## 📄 Paper Title

*“Unsupervised Clustering Uncovers Two Distinct Types of Fixational Eye-Movements in Dynamic Environments”*  
Submitted to **IEEE SPMB2025**  
Authors: N. W. Heinrich, A. Jahanian Najafabadi, and J. Perez-Osorio
